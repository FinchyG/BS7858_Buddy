What is BS7858 background screening?

The BS7858 background check aims to show your whereabouts for the past five years: whether in employment, claiming benefits, in education or training, outside of the UK, or other.
There are many documents that can verify each of these circumstances, and this website’s aim is to show you how to pass your BS7858 screening.
