Glossary

Statutory declaration

A document (usually prepared by your current employer) stating what you were doing during a certain period, that you would get signed and stamped at a solicitors (usually for a fee of between £5 to £10) that would cover that period to the BS7858 standard.

Gap reference

A person (should be a friend you are not related to, have not worked for, and do not live with) who has known you for a least five years and can verify what you were doing during a period that you have used documents to cover.

Benefits printout

A document that can be requested from the DWP that will confirm your benefits claims start and end dates over the last five years and can be used to cover those periods. Each different benefit type will require the requesting of its own specific printout.

Employment, Benefit and Pension (EBP) printout

A document that can be requested from HMRC that will confirm all your employment start and end dates over the last five years through tracing National Insurance contributions.
